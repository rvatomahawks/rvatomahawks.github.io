About

Mission Statement

Our mission at the RVA Tomahawks (Located: Richmond, Virginia) is to create cultivate second chance opportunity for you men who did or did not a sufficient GPA coming out of high school to move forward with pursuing a college education and potentially playing college football. Further, we are also seeking athletes who’s dreams may have faded of ever playing this great game and may have possibly never finished completing their high school education as well. We believe that everyone deserves a chance and we are excited to be in the posture to present individual athletes with the opportunity to bring about a greater quality of life for the individual while also helping them realize that “the sky is the limit” and that the possibilities within their reach are very real and tangible if they are willing to make a determined commitment while working hard to apply themselves properly.

The nature of the program

The nature of the program revolves around our tireless commitment to excellence in teaching the latest innovative skills and techniques to our athletes to empower them to play the game of football at the highest level achievable. We believe that we have one of the finest coaching staffs assembled in the state of Virginia and abroad.

Philosophy

Our philosophy at the RVA Tomahawks is that by fostering a culture base on ethical and moral values, we have the awesome privilege of not only raising up some fine football players but, we have the blessed opportunity to impact the world, one individual at a time by shaping our young men through the game of football and by mentoring them to bring about characteristics like honor, respect, accountability and integrity, faith coupled with belief to help them understand that anything is possible, and finally, leadership qualities so that they can lead a fulfilled life by reaching out and helping others, Further, we believe that these qualities empower our athletes to become effective models which is something that the world’s culture desperately needs at this very moment and time.

We Believe in Giving Back to our Community

Our team will be busy this upcoming season in surrounding communities by helping in various programs such as March of Dimes, Cancer Awareness, Food Banks, Domestic Violence, Toys for Tots, ect.
