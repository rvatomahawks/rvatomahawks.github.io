Current Coaches and Staff

Owner- Jerome "Rome" Jackson

From: Brookneal, VA

Coach Rome, has loved the game of football since a young boy, playing as a youth then playing into his high school years then into semi-pro. Was offered many scholarships but due to injury his dream of playing college ball was interrupted. Jerome played for many teams in Semi-Pro and has coached for the last five years from special team’s coach, assistant coach to head coach. After considering the time he has put into other teams his wife and he chose to start their own team. His dream is to give back the same way coaches gave back to him. His dream is to not only be an owner, but it is to be a mentor to each and every one of his players and truly make a difference in their lives. Community involvement is also very important to Coach Rome, it is truly the foundation of this team.

President- Shebeney Jackson

Shebeney Jackson is the President of the RVA Tomahawks. Her goal is for the team to become successful on the field as well as off of the field, her job is also to ensure the club promotes the participation and achievement of its team not only on the field but off of the field as well within the community and helping make a change. Mrs. Jackson ensures the club is run efficiently administratively, financially and socially to support all on-field activities. The President helps the committee priorities its goals and keeps the committee on track by working within the club’s framework. At an operational level, the major function of the President is to facilitate effective committee meetings, ensure committee members, team manager and coaches fulfil their responsibilities to the club.
